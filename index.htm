Short Bio

Currently I am a Ph.D. student of Department of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University and a member of Websoft Research Group. I received my B.Sc. degree in College of Computer and Information in June 2016 from Hohai University. In the same year, I was admitted to study for a M.Sc. degree in Nanjing University without entrance examination. In Sept. 2018, I started my Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Yuzhong Qu and Associate Prof. Wei Hu.


Research Interest

My research interests include knowledge graph (representation learning, entity alignment, relation prediction, etc.), applied machine learning and deep learning (recurrent neural networks, graph neural networks, etc.).
